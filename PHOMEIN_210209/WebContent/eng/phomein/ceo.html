I am Kim Dae-il, CEO of Daily King Co., Ltd..
Thank you for visiting Phomein.

Today of the 13th year,
we will not lose our own principles today

Since opening Jeongja Head Store in 2006, we have fulfilled our promise of ‘safe food ingredients and honest food’. We are steadily continuing it with the establishment of <PHO SEASON>, a Vietnam local corporation rice noodle manufacturing factory in 2015 and business agreement with <Hyundai Green Food> a comprehensive food company which introduced hazardous product blocking system in 2016.

In addition, we will be able to meet new rice noodles only at all stores of Phomein in 2019 as the result of 'our own principle'. Making a bowl of rice noodles only with new rice in the rice noodle market that depends on imported rice noodles made of old rice, we put care and sincerity which became thicker as the cooking process becomes more delicate.

Please experience the chewy Phomein New Rice Noodles which were born with the Phomein’s own low-temperature aging technique without any smell of rice noodles.

We promise fresh ingredients and healthy table every day without losing modesty even before our reputation of being the best for a long time. Thank you.

CEO of Daily King Co., Ltd. Kim Dae-il
